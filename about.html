About Us

Our Story

Hi, I'm Hannah - the groomer behind Huggs Pawly! I have a full-time job, but in my free time, I turn my little corner at home into a cozy space for grooming. It started as a hobby because I simply love being around animals and it's something I truly look forward to every day.

Nothing makes me happier than seeing furkids clean, comfy, and wagging their tails after a session. Every groom is done with lots of patience, love, and care, just like how I would treat my own.
